Good questions

Things people always ask us.

Do we need to be "technical" to work with you?+

Not at all. Most of our clients run businesses with nothing to do with tech — clinics, law firms, real estate, restaurants. We translate between "your business" and "the automation." You just need to know how your operation works.

What does a project actually cost?+

It depends on scope, and we're honest about that upfront. The audit is our entry point and is fixed-price so you can try us without risk. For build work, most small engagements land between a few thousand and low five-figures. We'll always give you a firm estimate before you commit.

How long until we see results?+

Our first automation usually goes live within 2 weeks. We build in small pieces, so you feel the impact quickly instead of waiting months for a big reveal.

What about our data and privacy?+

We take this seriously as a German-based consultancy under GDPR. We use privacy-first models where possible, never train external models on your data, and walk you through exactly what's being sent where. For regulated industries we can architect everything to stay inside your own environment.

Will AI replace our staff?+

That's not what we do. Our goal is to remove the boring parts of your team's work so they can focus on what actually requires a human — judgment, relationships, nuance. Most clients redeploy people, not let them go.

Do you work with businesses outside Germany?+

Yes — we're based in Mülheim an der Ruhr but work with SMEs globally, remotely. Calls happen over video, builds happen in your tools, and we schedule around your timezone.

Not sure where to start?
