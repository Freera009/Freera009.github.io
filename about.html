About us

Big-business AI, small-business prices.

Freera exists because the AI revolution is mostly happening at the top. The biggest companies get the best tools, the best consultants, and the best outcomes. We think that's backwards — and we're here to change it.

Our story

A short why.

AI tools are everywhere. New ones launch every week. And yet — walk into most small and mid-sized businesses and you'll find the same scene: a tired team, drowning in admin, wondering if any of this technology is actually meant for them.

That's the gap Freera was built to close. We take the capabilities that Fortune 500 companies pay seven-figure consultancies to implement, and we deliver them to businesses with 5 to 500 employees — at prices and timelines that make sense for their size.

No enterprise jargon. No six-month "transformation programmes." No overpromising what AI can do. Just a careful look at what your team spends its time on, an honest plan for what to automate first, and the hands-on work to actually make it happen.

We're consultants, builders, and — above all — pragmatists. If AI doesn't fit a problem, we'll tell you. If a cheaper tool would work just as well, we'll point you to it. We'd rather build a long relationship than close a short deal.

What we believe

Four principles we don't bend on.

01

Speak human.

If a client can't understand what we're doing, we've failed before we've started. Clear English, every meeting, every email, every deliverable. If we use a technical term, we define it.

02

Measure everything.

Every automation we build has a number attached — hours saved, errors reduced, revenue captured, response time cut. If we can't measure it, we're not building the right thing.

03

Small steps, fast wins.

We reject the "big reveal" model of consulting. You'll see working automations in weeks, not quarters. Small wins build momentum — and let us course-correct quickly if something isn't landing.

04

Your data, your rules.

Privacy isn't a checkbox for us. We architect every build around your data sensitivity — local models for regulated industries, clear retention policies, and total transparency on what goes where.
